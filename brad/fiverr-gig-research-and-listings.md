# PART 2: KEY MARKET INSIGHTS

## Pricing Strategy Recommendations
1. **Don't race to the bottom.** The $5-$20 tier is overcrowded with offshore sellers. Brad should price at mid-to-premium ($50-$500+).
2. **AI + automation combo commands highest premiums.** Combining n8n/Make with AI agents allows 2-3x pricing.
3. **Fiverr takes 20% from sellers.** Price accordingly: a $100 gig nets $80 after Fiverr's cut.
4. **Extras are where the money is.** Rush delivery (+$20-50), additional revisions (+$10), source code (+$50), ongoing support (+$30/month).

## SEO/Visibility Strategy
1. Title must start with "I will" (Fiverr's format).
2. Front-load the highest-volume keyword in the title.
3. Use all 5 tag slots with distinct, high-search-volume keywords.
4. Naturally weave keywords 2-3 times into the description (no stuffing).
5. Allow 30 days for new keywords to impact visibility.
6. Fiverr suggests popular tags based on your title -- use them.

## Competitive Advantages Brad Can Leverage
- **26 years at Verizon as Distinguished Architect** (enterprise credibility)
- **Existing production systems** (not theoretical -- working projects)
- **Full-stack capability** (Python + Node.js + Playwright + Puppeteer + APIs)
- **AI-native** (actually uses Claude in production, not just ChatGPT tutorials)
- **US-based** (many buyers prefer domestic sellers for communication/timezone)
